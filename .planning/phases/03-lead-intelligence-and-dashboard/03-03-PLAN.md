<objective>
Build the lead detail view: a full-page view showing all project information, an interactive Google Map with location pin, inferred equipment needs with confidence, equipment timeline with urgency windows, and source attribution.

Purpose: When a sales rep clicks a lead card in the feed, they see everything they need to decide whether to pursue the lead -- project details, where it is on a map, what equipment they might need and when, and where the data came from.

Output: Lead detail page at `/dashboard/leads/[id]` with map, timeline, equipment components, and detail-view smoke test.
</objective>
