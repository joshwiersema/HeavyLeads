**Rewrite `pagination.tsx`** to use cursor-based "Load More":

The component becomes a simple "Load More" button. It receives `nextCursor` and `hasMore` as props. When clicked, it appends `?cursor={nextCursor}` to the URL (preserving all other search params). The server will fetch the next batch.
