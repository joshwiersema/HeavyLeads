Build NWS storm alert and FEMA disaster declaration scraper adapters, wire them into the factory and pipeline, add a 25-point storm urgency scoring boost, and create a dedicated 30-minute cron route.

Purpose: Storm events are the killer feature for roofing subscribers. Fast hail/tornado alerts that convert to leads within 30 minutes of NWS issuance give roofers a competitive edge. FEMA disaster declarations surface demand signals for roofing and heavy equipment.

Output: Two new scraper adapters, updated factory, storm cron route, urgency scoring boost, and comprehensive tests.
