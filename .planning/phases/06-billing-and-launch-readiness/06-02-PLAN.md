       - Do NOT check subscription status (this is the key difference from (dashboard) layout)

       - Include a "Back to Dashboard" link at the top pointing to "/dashboard" (for users who already have a subscription and navigated here from settings)

       - This is a SERVER component
       - Import auth from "@/lib/auth", headers from "next/headers", getActiveSubscription from "@/lib/billing"
       - Get session, get activeOrganizationId
       - Call getActiveSubscription(activeOrganizationId)
       - If NO active subscription: render a "Get Started" card with pricing info ($X/mo + one-time setup fee), feature bullet points (Daily lead feed, Multi-source intelligence, Equipment matching, Email digests), and the SubscribeButton component passing organizationId
       - If active subscription: render BillingStatus component with subscription data, and ManageBillingButton below it
       - Page title: "Billing" with subtitle "Manage your subscription and payment details"
