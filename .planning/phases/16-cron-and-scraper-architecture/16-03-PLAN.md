<objective>
Create per-industry cron routes with staggered schedules and idempotency protection, add lead enrichment and expiration crons, and build a scraper health monitoring system.

Purpose: Move from a single monolithic daily scrape to independent per-industry pipelines that can run on different schedules, fail independently, and be monitored for health. Enrichment tags leads with cross-industry relevance after scraping. Expiration marks stale leads to keep the feed fresh. Health monitoring detects failing adapters before they silently stop producing leads.

Output: 5 per-industry cron routes, enrichment cron, expiration cron, health monitoring cron, staggered vercel.json schedule, comprehensive tests.
</objective>
