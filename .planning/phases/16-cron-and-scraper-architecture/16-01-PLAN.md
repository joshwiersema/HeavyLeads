<objective>
Replace the mutable global adapter registry with a pure factory pattern, add SHA-256 content-hash deduplication as a pre-insert fast path, integrate p-queue rate limiting for external APIs, and wire scraper_runs per-adapter tracking into the pipeline.

Purpose: Eliminate the race-condition-prone global Map registry that will cause duplicate/missing adapter runs when per-industry crons overlap; add content-hash dedup to supplement existing proximity dedup; prepare the pipeline for per-industry invocation with proper rate limiting and health tracking.

Output: Factory-based adapter system, content hash module, rate limiter module, pipeline with scraper_runs integration, updated cron/on-demand routes, comprehensive tests.
</objective>
